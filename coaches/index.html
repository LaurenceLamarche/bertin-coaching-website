Seb is an IFBB Pro himself. He strives to coach his athletes the way he always would have liked to be coached. Along with his background in engineering as a construction project manager, he has over 10 years of coaching experience building champions from the ground up.

His bodybuilding career spans over 12 years and 16+ shows, including a 54-week prep to earn his pro card, which humbled him, made him a better human and a better coach.

If you've ever been told to "push through" by someone who doesn't understand what emotional or physical struggles you're going through in the depths of prep, you can be sure that this won't be the case with Seb. You'll always get a fair, honest and empathetic coach who will do everything in his power to see you win.

Seb has never been genetically gifted, but through meticulous planning, strategy and hard work, he has been able to achieve his dreams of being a Pro athlete — and now prides himself on teaching others how to do the same. Whether you're just starting out or already pushing to reach the next level, he is no stranger to facing a challenge head on and bringing the best out of his athletes.
